# Day 1 — #90DaysOfDevOps

## My Understanding of DevOps

DevOps is a culture that connects development and operations teams to build, test, deploy, and manage applications efficiently. It focuses on automation, collaboration, CI/CD, and faster software delivery with reliability and scalability.

What fascinates me most about DevOps is the combination of Linux, Cloud, Networking, Automation, Docker, Kubernetes, and real-world problem solving.

---

## Why I Started Learning DevOps & Cloud

I completed my post-graduation (MCM) in 2019, but I could not properly explore my IT career at that time. However, I was always fascinated by cloud technologies, servers, infrastructure, and automation.

Recently, I cleared the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02) certification, which motivated me to restart my IT journey seriously and confidently.

Through this #90DaysOfDevOps challenge, I want to build strong practical skills, gain hands-on experience, and become job-ready in DevOps and Cloud.
